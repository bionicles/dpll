DPLL.js

An implementation of the Davis-Putnam-Logemann-Loveland (DPLL) algorithm for solving the CNF-SAT problem that runs on Node.js.

Here's a propositional logic formulae in conjunctive normal form:

(a ∨ b) ∧ (¬b ∨ c ∨ ¬d) ∧ (d ∨ ¬e)

Or in plain english:

(a or b) and (not b or c or not d) and (not e)

Now, how do we find the boolean values of a, b, c, d and e in order to the formula to be true?
